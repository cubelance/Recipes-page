Description

Popular pasta dish from Italy known and loved by many

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  1. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes
  2. Put ground beef, tomato sauce and garlic in a pan
  3. Use medium heat and stir for 15 minutes
  4. Take the pasta out and put it on a plate
  5. Add the sauce with everything
  6. Done!
